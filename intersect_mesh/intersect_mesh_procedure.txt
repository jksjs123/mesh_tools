
1) convert mesh files to irit format:

   mesh2irit d000_MERGED_FILTERED_MANIP.mesh > d000_MERGED_FILTERED_MANIP.itd
   mesh2irit cube_cutter.mesh > cube_cutter.itd

2) edit intersect_mesh.irt to operate on the desired irit mesh files.

3) run irit script (this might take several minutes):

   irit intersect_mesh.irt


4) convert result of above from irit format to mesh format:

   irit2mesh intersect_mesh_obj.itd > my_mesh_file.mesh

   or

irit2mesh intersect_mesh_obj.itd | Filtermesh -fixvertices -fixfaces > foo.mesh
